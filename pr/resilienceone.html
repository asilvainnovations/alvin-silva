An AI-powered chatbot designed to provide real-time, evidence-based guidance for personal resilience building. Built on the Resilience CASE Equation —a holistic, systems-thinking framework—the bot helps users assess, understand, and improve their resilience by interacting across four domains: Coping Capacity (C), Adaptability (A), Sensitivity Reduction (S), and Exposure Control (E) .

ResilienceOne combines the convenience of instant messaging with cutting-edge psychological science, offering personalized nudges, exercises, and micro-coaching tailored to the user’s current context, goals, and emotional state.

Whether you're a well-being professional, HR leader, or individual seeking growth, ResilienceOne offers personalized mental wellness support at scale —made possible by the trusted capabilities of Facebook Messenger
