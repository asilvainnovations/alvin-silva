Q1: What is the CASE Equation in "Personal Resilience: The Path to Oneness"? 

A1: The CASE Equation is a practical framework for building resilience, defined as R = CASE:

  • Coping Capacity Development (C): Enhancing one's ability to manage stress and adversity.
  • Adaptability Enhancement (A): Improving flexibility and the ability to adjust to new circumstances.
  • Sensitivity Reduction (S): Reducing vulnerability to stressors.
  • Exposure Control (E): Minimizing exposure to harmful or disruptive influences.

Q2: Why is personal resilience important in today's world? 

A2: Personal resilience is crucial for navigating uncertainty, building sustainable businesses, and fostering community well-being. It helps individuals adapt without losing themselves, evolve without breaking, and thrive through mindful alignment with life's journey.


Q3: How does "Personal Resilience: The Path to Oneness" redefine resilience?
